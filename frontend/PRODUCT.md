# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

DeFi participants on Stellar looking to deposit into or manage positions in RAW$ pools. They arrive from the marketing landing page (evaluating the protocol, connecting a wallet) and then move into the app surfaces (dashboard, pools, deposit flows) to transact. Mobile usage is expected alongside desktop, since wallet apps are often mobile-first.

## Product Purpose

RAW$ is a stablecoin/yield protocol live on Stellar mainnet. The landing page sells trust and clarity of the protocol; the app (dashboard, pools, deposits) lets connected users track and manage positions. Success looks like a visitor understanding the protocol and connecting a wallet, then navigating fluidly between pools/dashboard without friction on any device size.

## Brand Personality

Technical, minimal, trustworthy. Terminal/dev-tool inflected — dark noir palette, monospace numerals and labels, serif display headings, low visual noise. Confidence comes from precision and restraint, not loud claims.

## Anti-references

Not generic "SaaS-cream" landing pages, not gradient-heavy crypto-hype sites, not bouncy/playful motion. Avoid diluting the noir/mono technical identity with soft, rounded, consumer-app styling.

## Design Principles

- Precision over decoration — the interface reads as a trustworthy technical instrument, not a marketing gimmick.
- One consistent chrome — landing header and in-app navbar should feel like the same product at every breakpoint.
- Restraint in color — cherry/cotton/noir palette carries brand weight; new UI should extend it, not introduce new hues.
- Mobile is not an afterthought — wallet-connected users frequently arrive on mobile; navigation must be fully usable, not just shrunk.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No explicit WCAG level specified; default to WCAG AA (contrast ≥4.5:1 body text, ≥3:1 large text/UI). Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for any menu open/close transitions. Ensure the collapsible navbar is keyboard-operable and has correct `aria-expanded`/`aria-label` semantics.
